Welcome

I’m Serena, a computer science student at the College of Wooster interested in using computational methods to support cognition, identity exploration, and social understanding. My Independent Study uses Big Five personality data to estimate Jungian cognitive functions and apply PCA to investigate whether these functions collapse into fewer meaningful dimensions. I also work in STEM education and mentorship, and I plan to pursue a PhD in cognitive science, computational neuropsychology, and interpretable AI.
