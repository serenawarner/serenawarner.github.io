üíâ Diabetes Diagnosis Analysis

For my Applied Statistics course, we had to do a project analyzing a dataset. I chose a dataset using the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) collected by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) focused on analyzing which medical and lifestyle factors can impact having a diabetes diagnosis. Through logistic regression, I found that diabetes diagnosis has relationships with age, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, physical activity, heavy alcohol consumption, and income.

Skills: R, Stats, Data Science
